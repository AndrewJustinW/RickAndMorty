MEET THE FAMILY

Rick Sanchez

  • Age: 70

  • Universe of Origin: Unknown. Assumed to be Earth C-137

  • Occupation: Scientist, Inventor, Arms salesman

  • Family:
    Beth Smith (Daughter)
    Jerry Smith (Son-In-Law)
    Summer Smith (Grand Daughter)
    Morty Smith (Grandson)

  • A genius alcoholic scientist who displays reckless and nihilistic behavior.

  • Had been mysteriously missing from the family for a total of 14 years.

  • Seems to be aware he is a cartoon character.

  • Proves to be the only Rick that actually cares about Morty

Morty Smith

  • Age: 14 (Student)

  • Universe of Origin: Earth C-137

  • Family:
    Beth Smith (Mother)
    Jerry Smith (Father)
    Summer Smith (Sister)
    Rick Sanchez (Grand Father)

  • Morty is an anxious teen based on Marty McFly from Back to the Future.

  • Believed to have or have had a learning disability.

  • The grandson of Rick and is often forced to tag along on his various misadventures.

  • May sometimes act spineless, but has shown bravery and quick-wittedness in the face of adversity.

  • Despite his malleable nature, Morty has the strength to be able to stand up to Rick in the Multi-Verse.

Summer Smith

  • Age: 17 (Student)

  • Family:
    Beth Smith (Mother)
    Jerry Smith (Father)
    Morty Smith (Brother)
    Rick Sanchez (Grand Father)

  • Universe of Origin: Earth C-137

  • Summer has the personality traits of a typical American teenager.

  • Has shown jealousy of the close relationship between her grandfather Rick and her brother Morty.

  • At times can display sociopathic and narcissistic behavior, like her grandfather.

  • Although Summer is not as scientifically-minded as her grandfather Rick, she does possess an above-average intelligence.

Beth Smith

  • Age: 34

  • Occupation: Veterinary Surgeon

  • Family:
    Summer Smith (Daughter)
    Jerry Smith (Husband)
    Morty Smith (Son)
    Rick Sanchez (Father)

  • An intelligent, ambitious, and independent woman who suffers from a variety of deep-seated insecurities.

  • Beth came to idolize her father and blamed her mother for "chasing off the dad with the guts to leave".

  • At some point of her childhood, Beth became an insane and psychotic little girl.

  • She has generally considered her family her first priority and has "defined her life" around her marriage to Jerry.

  • Beth has a superiority complex, believing herself at times to be above others as a way of hiding her insecurities, and has extended this behavior to those such as her son Morty.

Jerry Smith

  • Age: 34 (Unemployed)

  • Origin: Unkown (May have been switched with another universe's Jerry)

  • Family:
    Summer Smith (Daughter)
    Beth Smith (Wife)
    Morty Smith (Son)
    Rick Sanchez (Father-In-Law)
  • Seen as the least valuable and weakest member of the family

  • Frequently battles with bouts of insecurity and jealousy, being constantly reminded that his marriage is on rocky terms.

  • Has a habit of quitting when things get too difficult.

  • Constantly finds himself lacking compared to his father-in-law Rick's intelligence and resents him to a degree.

  • Jerry puts up with Rick to a degree because he knows how much it means to Beth that Rick is in her life after being absent for so long.
